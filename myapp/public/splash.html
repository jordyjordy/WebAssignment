REVERSI

Try to fill the board with as many chips of your colours as you can!

A chip must be placed so that there exists a chip of another colour between it and an anchcoring of the player's colour chip. The chips between with another colour will be flipped. This can go horizontally, vertically and diagonally.

The game is over when there are no moves left or when all chips have been captured by one colour.
